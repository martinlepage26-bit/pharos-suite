# Example Website Branding Direction

## Hero mood

Calm, severe, and legible under pressure.

## Hierarchy

One clear thesis, one trust bridge, one primary call to action.

## Typography system

Display serif or sharp grotesk for authority, paired with a disciplined sans for body copy.

## Color system

Dark field, one controlled warm accent, one cool structural accent.

## Trust architecture

Method, credentials, deliverables, and process appear before decorative storytelling.

## Interaction tone

Subtle motion, no theatrical glows, and buttons that feel decisive rather than promotional.
