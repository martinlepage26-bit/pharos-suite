# Strong Identity Brief

- Audience: Compliance leaders and procurement reviewers at regulated enterprises
- Offer: Governance design that converts abstract AI policy into reviewable operating controls
- Differentiation: Inspection-ready logic and documents, not generic ethics messaging
- Buying context: High-trust sale under scrutiny, often late in the cycle
- Trust requirement: Serious, literate, defensible, stable
- Adjectives: severe, lucid, calm, authoritative
- Avoid: startup neon, luxury codes, generic “future” branding
- Visual thesis: controlled typography, restrained accent color, one symbolic logic, strong document behavior
