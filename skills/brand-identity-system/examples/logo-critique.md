# Example Logo Critique

## Verdict

Strategically close but formally overbuilt.

## Strongest signal

The mark suggests orientation and authority quickly.

## Strategic mismatch

Glow, depth effects, and secondary symbols push the identity toward entertainment rather than governance.

## Operational risk

The mark will collapse in small sizes and reproduce inconsistently across decks and documents.

## Redesign priority

Remove secondary symbolism, simplify the contour count, and tighten the wordmark relationship.
