# AI Governance — Brand System

## Brand
- **Primary mark**: Serif wordmark "AI Governance" + descriptor "Practice & Research" + author "Martin Lepage"
- **Secondary mark**: ML monogram (favicon/watermark)
- **Optional symbol**: Minimal NE compass (classical, restrained)

## Color System
Black + purple on white/off-white only.

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Purple | `#4B2ABF` | Buttons, links, accents, active states |
| Deep Purple | `#1A1033` | CTA gradient backgrounds, dark sections |
| Black | `#0B0F1A` | Headlines, body text |
| Off-white | `#F6F7FB` | Page background |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, inputs |

## Typography
| Role | Font | Fallback |
|------|------|----------|
| Headlines/Logo | Source Serif 4 | Georgia, serif |
| Body/UI | Inter | system-ui, sans-serif |

## Rules
- **Backgrounds**: Only white/off-white. No photos behind logo.
- **Shadows**: Soft, short, downward only. (`0 2px 8px rgba(11,15,26,0.04)`)
- **Glow**: Halo behind text only, never neon edging.
- **Mood**: Institutional authority, academic rigor, operational defensibility.
